# Prompt – Version 2

You are a senior digital marketing strategist and AI educator with 10+ years of experience.

We are launching an online AI course for complete beginners who want to learn Prompt Engineering and Generative AI.

Write a persuasive, human-sounding landing page.

Goals:
- Make the tone natural and conversational.
- Reduce robotic or overly dramatic language.
- Add one short relatable learner scenario or story.
- Include 2–3 long-tail SEO phrases such as:
  “learn prompt engineering for beginners”
  “how to use AI tools without coding”
  “generative AI course online”

Structure the landing page as:
1. Headline
2. Short conversational introduction
3. What You’ll Learn (bullet points)
4. Why This Course Is Different
5. Who This Is For
6. Short learner scenario (3–4 lines)
7. FAQ section (2 questions)
8. Strong but natural Call to Action

Constraints:
- Avoid excessive emojis.
- Avoid hype-heavy or fear-based language.
- Keep sentences simple and human.
